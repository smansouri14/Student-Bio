About Me


I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona. After highschool I attended the University of Arizona where I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Public Health, with a minor in Business in 2014.

I am an active member in the community participating as a health education intern with the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.

I love to travel all over the world. Places I have been include Ireland, England, Iran, Hawaii, Portland, Seattle, Toronto, and many more. I also enjoy cooking food for my wife and I. I even brew my own cold brew coffee.

Additionally, I love sports and I am a huge Arsenal football fan (aka soccer). I try and not miss a game even if this means waking up at 4:00 AM to watch.
